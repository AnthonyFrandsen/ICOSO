ICOSO

Who we are

ICOSO is a small American business dedicated to making perfect dice crafted to the specifications of our customers.

Our company was founded by people passionate for tabletop gaming. We understand the importance of aesthetics for roleplaying. We make products for players like us that search for the perfect die every time we need to make a roll.

We have gathered a wide range of materials and colors, in addition to developing many brand new trademarked designs to fit the mood of any tabletop scenario.

Whether you need a metal die with runic numbers for your dwarf's axe swing, or eight electric-blue crystal-style dice for that epic lightning bolt, we can make the dice that suit the roll.
