Commissions

We understand that the desire for the perfect dice can lead to dissatisfaction with any out-of-the-box solution. Our customizer offers many solutions that mass-produced dice just don't offer, but if your idea can't be created through our customizer service, we'll happily take a commission to make your perfect dice.

Please leave your name, email address, and description of your commission request and we will contact you for further details and pricing.
